Contact information

These pages are maintained by Jonas Wahlbeck (jwahlbec(a)cc.hut.fi). Comments and suggestions about the pages can be also sent directly to the club : karate(a)list.tky.fi

Karate club Shoto ry operates in the Helsinki and Espoo area in close co-operation with TKY-KDS and LKS-KDS. TKY-KDS and LKS-KDS can in practice be considered as parts of Shoto ry. The courses are arranged by Shoto ry and the membership fee is included in the course fees. The main instructor of the club is Peter Nystén.

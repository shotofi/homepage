Camp schedule 2012

May - June Solvalla

CAMPS

Camps/Weekend courses are arranged during the school year usually at least once a month and one additional camp is held during the summer. The course fee for one weekend camp is usually 20 euros or about 60 euros for courses held by a foreign (imported) teacher. To attend camps you have to have paid your yearly membership fee of 20 euros to Suomen Karate-Do-Shotokai (SKDS).

The Solvalla long weekend course is held at the end of May. It is the main event of finnish Shotokai and Shoto-Budo clubs. The course fee is about 130 euros for SKDS members (170 euros for non-members) but slightly less than half of it is usually reimbursed by the club for those that attend. The course fee includes breakfast, dinner, lodging and three training sessions per day. A separate letter is sent about the camp details each spring to members of SKDS.

You can participate in the camps and weekend courses after completing the beginner's course. You can try to graduate during the camps by informing about your intent at the start of the camp at the latest and by paying the graduation fee of 10 euros. New grades are announced during the first regular practice session after the camp.

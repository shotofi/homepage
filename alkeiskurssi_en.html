The essence of the Shotokai-style is movement. The point of the practice is not so much the repetitious practice of singular details but to develope a usable body. This takes concentration and knowing your own body. The style does not include any competitions. We don't demand knowledge in terminology but you'll soon be familiar with the most common terms.

Take loose exercise clothing with you already to the first practice. You don't necessarily have to be in particularly good basic condition as you'll be able to get in shape during the course. A karate uniform (gi) is not obligatory during the beginner's course.

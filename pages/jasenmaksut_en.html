MEMBERSHIP FEES

Shoto Ry

The membership fee for Shoto ry is 40 euros for each semester. The beginner's course fee includes the Shoto ry membership fee for the semester in question. The account number for the fee is FI22 5800 1320 0268 41 (OP/OKO). Detailed instructions about membership fees is sent to email in each semester.

Suomen Karate Do Shotokai (SKDS Finland)

If you want to participate in the camps arranged by Suomen Karate-Do-Shotokai you should pay the yearly SKDS membership fee of 20 euros.

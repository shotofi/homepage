THE VIRTUAL HOME OF SHOTO RY

Martial art with self-defence oriented stand-up fighting

Welcome to practice shotokai-style karate with us. We work in cooperation with Unisport.

The atmosphere during our practices is relaxed - the discipline comes from trainer's own motivation, not from outside. Shouting kiai does not belong to our style, and the level of physical contact is kept on appropriate level.

In addition to practices we gather around for example to watch movies and play board games.

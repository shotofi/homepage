TRAINING PLACES

Kaapelitehdas

Tallberginkatu 1, Helsinki

Enter from staircase D, dressing rooms are on the 3rd floor, up one floor turn right and again right at the end of a long hallway to get to the practice hall.

Lockers in changing rooms require one euro coin as a deposit.

UniDojo - Pohjoinen Hesperiankatu 25

The entrance to the practise hall is through the gate in the corner of Pohjoinen Hesperiankatu and Välskärinkatu. Go through the inner yard to the back. There is a doorbell by the door. The dressing rooms are on the right side and the training hall on the left side. You go to the training hall through a staircase and then through a door. The training hall is at the end of the corridor behind some sliding doors.

Lockers in changing rooms do not have locks, so it is advised to bring your own padlock.

GRADUATION

Graduation takes place in camp or in own club's graduation practice. After green belt all graduations happens in camps and Dan-graduations in Solvalla Main Camp.

One must discuss with own club's instructor about graduation before attending graduation. The price of graduation is 10 euros, and it is paid only once for each rank.

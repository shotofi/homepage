Membership fees

Shoto Ry

The membership fee for Shoto ry is 40 euros for each semester. The beginner's course fee includes the Shoto ry membership fee for the semester in question. The account number for the fee is OP/OKO 580013-226841.

Suomen Karate Do Shotokai

If you want to participate in the camps arranged by Suomen Karate-Do-Shotokai you should pay the yearly SKDS membership fee of 20 euros. All graduations after green belt are held at camps only. The graduation fee is 10 euros.

You can participate in the camps only after completing the beginner's course,in other words, after the participant has sufficient basic skills to be able to participate meaningfully in the camps. You can join the club also without taking the beginner's course if you have previous karate training and you think you are able to start with the regular practices straight away. Special cases are dealt on person by person basis. Ask the instructors if you are unsure on how to proceed.

TKY-KDS

THE VIRTUAL HOME PAGE OF TKY KARATE DO SHOTOKAI & SHOTO RY

The karate club Shoto ry arranges karate practices together with TKY Karate Do Shotokai. Karate Do Shotokai in the Helsinki and Espoo area. Our club members are mostly university students.

Our karate practice consists of practices, camps, sauna nights and other activities. The club spirit is concentrated but nicely relaxed - we don't yell or make contact on purpose (these things have been known to happen occasionally).

Style descriptions can give you some idea about our style.

General admin info is also available (Club officials, fees, regulations, etc.) and there are some karate pictures, mostly from camps.

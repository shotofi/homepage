Interesting material related to martial arts

If you are considering starting to practice a martial art, it is worthwile to take the time to get to know something of the different styles and clubs and take a moment to think about it. All kinds of information on this subject is available on the web:

  • Advice about choosing a martial art (thanks to Merja Tornikoski)
  • Newbie Guide, The Newbie Guide to Martial Arts Training
  • Thinking about switching styles and joining our club?
  • Shoto ry also has a mailing list, which is mainly meant for announcements, info and other local use.
